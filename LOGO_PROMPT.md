# Logo Design Prompt for Lens

## Brand Overview
**Name:** Lens  
**Tagline:** See Your Idea Clearly  
**Industry:** AI-powered product validation SaaS  
**Target Audience:** Entrepreneurs, product managers, indie hackers, VCs  

## Design Requirements

### Core Concept
Create a logo that represents:
- **Focus & Clarity** - Like looking through a lens
- **Market Insight** - Seeing what others miss
- **Precision** - Accurate validation
- **Modern Technology** - AI-powered, cutting-edge

### Visual Elements to Consider
1. **Lens/Aperture Symbol** - Camera lens, focus rings, or aperture blades
2. **Circle/Crosshair** - Target, focus point, precision
3. **Eye/Vision** - Seeing clearly, insight
4. **Abstract Focus Lines** - Converging lines, depth of field
5. **Minimalist Dot** - Single focus point

### Style Guidelines
- **Minimalist** - Clean, simple, scalable
- **Modern** - Tech-forward, contemporary
- **Professional** - Trustworthy, enterprise-ready
- **Versatile** - Works in color, B&W, small sizes
- **Memorable** - Instantly recognizable

### Color Palette
- **Primary:** `#2563EB` (Blue - trust, technology)
- **Secondary:** `#7C3AED` (Purple - innovation, AI)
- **Monochrome:** Black/white for versatility

### Typography
- **Font Style:** Clean sans-serif (Inter, System UI, or custom)
- **Weight:** Medium to Bold for impact
- **Case:** Title case or all lowercase (modern feel)
- **Kerning:** Slightly increased for readability

## Inspiration References

### Similar Logos to Study
- **Figma** - Clean, geometric
- **Linear** - Minimalist, tech
- **Vercel** - Modern, abstract
- **Notion** - Simple, memorable
- **Stripe** - Professional, gradient

### What to Avoid
- ❌ Overly complex details
- ❌ Too many colors
- ❌ Generic "eye" icons
- ❌ Clip-art style
- ❌ Hard to read fonts
- ❌ Too small for favicon use

# Lens - Brand Guidelines

## Brand Identity

**Name:** Lens  
**Tagline:** See Your Idea Clearly  
**Mission:** Validate product ideas in 48 hours, not 48 weeks

---

## Visual Identity

### Logo Concept
A minimalist lens/aperture symbol representing:
- Focus and clarity
- Market insight
- Precision validation
- Professional quality

### Color Palette

| Name | Hex | Usage |
|------|-----|-------|
| Primary Blue | `#2563EB` | Brand, buttons, links |
| Secondary Purple | `#7C3AED` | Accents, AI features |
| Dark Gray | `#1E293B` | Text, headers |
| Light Gray | `#F8FAFC` | Backgrounds |
| Success Green | `#10B981` | Positive scores, validation |
| Warning Orange | `#F59E0B` | Caution, attention |
| Error Red | `#EF4444` | Errors, failed validations |

## Logo Variations

1. **Primary** - Full color (blue + purple)
2. **Dark mode** - White/light gray
3. **Monochrome** - Single color for print
4. **Icon only** - For favicons, app icons
5. **Horizontal** - Logo + text side by side
6. **Vertical** - Logo above text

---

## Do's and Don'ts

**DO:**
- Use clear space around logo (min 50px)
- Maintain aspect ratio
- Use official colors only
- Keep it simple and clean

**DON'T:**
- Stretch or distort
- Change colors arbitrarily
- Add effects (shadows, gradients)
- Use on busy backgrounds
- Rotate or flip

---

## Quick Start

1. Download logo files from `public/`
2. Add favicon to `public/favicon.ico`
3. Update `app/layout.tsx` with brand name
4. Update hero section in `app/page.tsx`
5. Test on light and dark backgrounds
